Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

At EchoCustom, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how our application handles your data.

1. Data Collection

EchoCustom is designed to function primarily offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information to external servers.

  • Notification Data: Our Notification Listener Service processes notifications locally on your device to trigger custom sounds. This data is never uploaded.
  • Contact Information: Access to contacts is used solely to identify incoming calls and apply custom ringtones. We do not sync your contacts.
  • System State: Battery, brightness, and connectivity states are monitored locally for widget updates.

2. Voice & Speech Recognition (v4.1 Beta)

The Maid Voice Assistant is designed with a privacy-first architecture:

  • 100% On-Device Processing: All speech recognition and wake-word ("Echo") detection are performed locally using on-device engines. We do not use cloud-based recognition APIs.
  • No Audio Storage: Your voice is never recorded, stored, or cached on the device's storage.
  • No Transmission: No audio data or transcriptions are ever transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

3. ML Intelligence (v4.0)

Our ML-powered triage (Language ID and Entity Extraction) operates entirely within the device's local sandbox. Notification content is processed in real-time to provide smart hints but is never uploaded, shared, or stored persistently.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to provide its core features:

  • Notification Access: To detect incoming alerts from other apps.
  • Microphone: Required for the Voice Assistant wake-word and command recognition (processed 100% locally).
  • Read/Write Contacts: To manage group-based ringtones.
  • Write Settings: To change system ringtones dynamically.
  • Query All Packages: To list installed apps for audio mapping.

5. Security

All configuration data, including app-to-sound mappings and contact groups, is stored securely in your device's private storage using Android's standard security protocols.

6. Third-Party Services

EchoCustom does not include third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Your device usage remains private to you.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
